analog photography by alec

one time i was in nyc with some friends and one of them pulled out a 35mm camera.
she took pictures of us throughout the night and afterwards i forgot about it entirely.
a few weeks later those pictures made it back to me - they were washed out, grainy and the date stamp glowed in the corner.

i immediately fell in love with those photos.

these pictures are a product of that spark of inspiration.
